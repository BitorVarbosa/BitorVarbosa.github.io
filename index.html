PROJECTS

Master Lemon

Unity PC Xbox Nintendo Switch PS5 Steam 2025

I worked as Technical Lead for the engineering team on this narrative adventure game inspired by a true story. Players learn words from various languages which they can use as game mechanics to craft items, teleport, move objects and more. Winner of Games For Change América Latina Award in 2022.

2048 Bosses

Unity PC Steam 2025

A puzzle roguelike game that uses your 2048 score to deliver powerful attacks while letting the Player choose a Character and learn skills which will help you navigate the danger and create interesting synergies. This is a solo project and I'm currently setting up the Steam page.

Idle Star Zoo: Animals Tycoon

Unity Android iOS Free 2021

A mobile idle game about managing an alien zoo, rescuing animals and taking care of their habitats. The game also features many minigames and seasonal content. I developed new features such as breeding and hundreds of bugfixes.

Card Battle Kingdom

Unity Android iOS Free 2023

A pvp real time card battling game. I was tasked with changing core game mechanics, such as removing the fixed team composition in order to turn the characters into playable cards during battle. I spent a few months implementing changes for a second launch of the game.

Migus

Unity WebGL Free 2020

Awarded first place winner on a national game jam happening over one weekend, so in 48 hours we came up with and idea and developed a functional game. Manage resources from 4 districts with different roles in order to upgrade and defend your fortress.

Fire Survivors

Unity Android Free 2022

A hypercasual game in the "Survivors" genre. You're a firefigther extinguishing hordes of flaming enemies and levelling up. Each level grants you 3 options of power ups for your character.

Runniets

Unity WebGL Free 2018

A game we made for college in under a month. The game is a satire of Philip K. Dick's book Second Variety. You are a soviet super soldier tasked with investigating the reign of American machines. The game is available for free on Newgrounds.

Past Persistence

Awarded second place winner in a Newgrounds game jam. Over two weeks I learned how to code in the Haxe language and developed a fun and simple game where each death spawns a permanent ghost version of you, but dying too much increases the Curse which can consume you forever.
